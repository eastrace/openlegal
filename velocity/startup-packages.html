Get Started

Form your corporate entity (LLC or C-corp) and prepare the legal documentation. The package includes the government registration/filing° fee, which vary by state/municipality. Any other government or third-party fees are not included.

  • Articls of Incorporation (C-corp)
  • By-laws (C-corp)
  • Memorandum regarding C-Corp formation, explaining basic C-corp entity structures, tax benefits/implications, and maintenance of the corporate form
  • Form SS-4 for obtaining an EIN from the IRS and state tax application
  • Corporate Minute Book containing all formation documents

  • Founder Stock Issuances (for up to four founders)
