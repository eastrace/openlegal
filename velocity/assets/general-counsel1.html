How it works

openlegal™ General Counsel is available in increments of 10 hours. It is up to the client to choose the amount of time that fits the size of their business. For small businesses with relatively little transactional work, 10-30 hours should be sufficient. For a larger business with routine contract work, 40-60 hours may be in order. Our attorneys work with clients to find the best fit at the best value proposition.

Advantages for Businesses Without In-House Counsel

openlegal™ General Counsel functions similar to an in-house legal team, but without the added overhead. This is the perfect solution for a business with routine legal needs, but without a large legal budget. Let openlegal™ General Counsel handle your headaches so you can handle your business.

Advantages for In-House Counsel

openlegal™ General Counsel is available to function as your safety-valve. For businesses that already have an in-house team or general counsel, openlegal™ is the perfect solution for variable workflow. Simply purchase the number of hours that fit your needs, and stop worrying about whether you should hire another associate. Then, when a surge in work occurs, simply pass it over to openlegal™ for processing. Before long, you will feel like our attorneys are yours--without the overhead, of course.

What Happens if More Time is Needed

No worries. Simply contact openlegal™ and we will work with you to cover the remainder of the quarter. Typically, we offer extended coverage pro rata.

Note: openlegal™ General Counsel does not include services covered by other practice areas, unless indicated above. This means that legal needs related to litigation, intellectual property protection, or startup formation are not typically included. Such services will be billed as a separate openlegal™ package where indicated. openlegal™ General Counsel is designed to cover routine transactional needs typically handled by in-house legal staff.
