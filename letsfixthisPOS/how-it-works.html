How it works

Our Unique Approach

You're Running a High-Growth Company

openlegal™ was designed to support high-growth companies looking to gain predictability and control their legal spend while getting easy access to pragmatic and high-quality legal advice, fixed prices and transparency. The right fit: Companies having a diverse set of partners, projects, deals and contractors/employees, spending on average at least $1,000+ on legal services per month.

You're Tired of the Current Legal Model

openlegal™ is a top-to-bottom rebuild of the current law firm business model, utilizing technology to allow you to buy on-demand, flat fee general counsel legal services, flat fee transactional intellectual property & patent services, and flat fee litigation services. The net effect for you is predictability and decreased legal spend -- typically 30-50% savings over hiring a typical law firm -- with highly skilled attorneys.

Engage

Review our openlegal™ Packages. Contact us. Our team will help you figure out which openlegal™ packages are right for your business. You sign an engagement agreement detailing rights, responsibilities and package details. And we're off to the races.

Deploy

Your openlegal™ attorney(s) will begin working for you & your business, utilizing the best in online project management software and legal practice management and docketing software. You will always have access to your client file at any time to see outstanding project status, set deadlines or review documents. We're building better attorney/client relationships, one piece of technology at a time.
